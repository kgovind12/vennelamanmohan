I'm Vennela, nice to meet you.

Hey there! I'm Vennela, a Mechanical Engineering graduate who has redirected her career path toward Design. Inspired by the words of one of my favorite authors, Sidney Sheldon, I intend on leaving the earth a better place than when I arrived. And I believe I could do that by improving the lives of others through design, and sometimes, art. I’m a self-taught designer who believes that learning is a lifelong journey. I enjoy working from ideation to development. In my leisure, I indulge myself in reading, which has become a place to explore my tastes and choices and build an understanding of the dynamic society around me.
My passion for reading encouraged me to explore the field of writing. This made me more empathetic of human emotions. The urge to pour all the pain, sorrow, happiness, excitement, and many more emotions into a collection of impactful words has led me to start a blog too. Besides that, I review books, write on current social events, and even paint sometimes.
